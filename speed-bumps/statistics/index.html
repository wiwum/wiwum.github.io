Here is some data I collected about the speed bumps on Jester Blvd:

The speedbumps are on a curve, so measuring them is somewhat complex. I used a measuring wheel to gather this data:


at their furthest apart (on the arc), they are 511.5 ft apart.


at their closest together (one the arc), they are 510 ft apart.


the speed bumps are 7 feet long and 6 feet wide.


the distance between the speedbumps in each lane is 3ft 10in.


the distance between the speedbumps in the middle of the two lanes is 4ft 11in.


the speedbumps are not flat. (see pictures of speedbumps)
